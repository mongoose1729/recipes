SOFT SUGAR CAKES

4 C. flour 1 ½ cup sugar
1 tsp. B. Powder 2 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. soda 2 eggs
¾ tsp. salt ¾ cup sour cream
1 cup. soft butter

Cream butter & sugar. Add eggs & beat. Sift first four ingredients together. Add flour mixture & sour cream alternately.

Drop on greased cookie sheet.

Bake about 10 mins at 400 degrees.
